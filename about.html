Our History

65+ Years of Trust in Orange County

Angels Bail Bonds was founded in 1958 and has grown to become one of the most trusted bail bond agencies in Southern California. Families in Huntington Beach, Newport Beach, Costa Mesa, Fountain Valley, and throughout Orange County have relied on us during their most difficult moments.

We serve the Huntington Beach Police Department Jail area and know every aspect of the local jail system — from booking procedures to typical release timelines. When you call us, you're speaking to an experienced agent who knows Huntington Beach and can give you real answers, not call-center scripts.

Our reputation has been built entirely on results. We don't advertise heavily — most of our clients come from referrals by attorneys, previous clients, and Orange County professionals who trust our work.
